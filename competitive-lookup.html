SSaporo Quick Reference — Differentiators & One-Liners ▼
Key differentiators
01 — SCALE
In-memory graph engine processes billions of attack paths; re-evaluated on a schedule (daily by default). Don't say "real-time."
02 — CHOKEPOINTS
Names the 5–10 fixes that collapse the most Tier-0 paths. Minimum effort, maximum risk reduction.
03 — HYBRID
On-prem AD + Entra + AWS + Okta + ADCS in one native attack graph. Not bolted together.
04 — BUSINESS-SAFE
Business-Impact safety net warns whether a fix disrupts legitimate access before it's applied.
05 — OUTCOMES
~27M paths eliminated day one · −80% attack surface / 12 mo · 75–95% of Tier-0 paths closed via 5–10 fixes · 5–10× hidden Tier-0 revealed.
MARKET
71% of orgs hit by an identity breach last year; 67% of ransomware started with identity (Sophos, 2026). ~$13.7B → ~$33B PEM by 2029.
One-liners
SDR / cold outreach
"Saporo maps every attack path across your identity environment — AD, Entra, AWS, Okta — and tells you the 5 changes that eliminate 80% of your risk. Most enterprises have hundreds of millions of paths they've never seen."
CISO discovery
"How confident are you that you'd know which identity misconfigurations in your environment could be chained together to reach a Domain Controller?"
Technical champion
"Most tools give you a list of findings. Saporo gives you a graph — the same view an attacker has — and then shows you the chokepoints where one fix collapses millions of paths."
vs. CrowdStrike / EDR / "we have detection covered"
"Those tools are excellent at detection and response. Saporo works upstream — it finds and eliminates the attack paths before they're exploited. Complementary, not competitive."
Qualify in 5 questions
01 · When is the tool used?
Before vs. during the attack — Saporo: before (preemptive exposure reduction).
02 · Which perimeter?
External-facing vs. internal — Saporo: internal (identity is the perimeter).
03 · Which assets?
Only the vulnerable vs. all identities — Saporo: every identity.
04 · Which identity problem?
Authn · Authz · Permissions · Lifecycle — Saporo: permissions & privileges (ISPM).
05 · What kind of graph?
Investigation · Access · Attack — Saporo: the attack graph.
